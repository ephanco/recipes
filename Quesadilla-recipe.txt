2 flour tortillas
1/2 cup shredded cheese, taco or mexican
2T taco sauce

Turn on quesadilla cokker.
Sprinkle cheese onto one tortilla.
Place 2nd tortilla over cheese.

Place into quesadialla cooker.
Wait about 5 minutes until teh tortilla shrinks a bit and reaches desired toast level.

Remove from tortilla cooker. Turn cooker off.
Splice quesadilla into wedges. Add taco sauce on the side. Dip quesadilla wedges into taco sauce and enjoy.
